## Windows to Linux Migration Guide for Europe (France 🇫🇷 Included)

Migrating from Windows to Linux is becoming increasingly popular across Europe, especially in France (*de plus en plus populaire*). With growing concerns about privacy (RGPD), cost, and performance, Linux offers a powerful alternative.

---

## 🇪🇺 Why Europeans Are Switching to Linux

- ✅ GDPR-friendly (*respect de la vie privée*)
- ✅ Free and open-source (*logiciel libre*)
- ✅ Better performance on older devices
- ✅ Strong community support in Europe

💡 In France, public institutions are actively encouraging open-source adoption.

---

## 🐧 Best Linux Distributions for Beginners (Europe-Friendly)

- **Ubuntu** – Best all-around choice (*très populaire en France*)
- **Linux Mint** – Windows-like interface
- **Zorin OS** – Designed for Windows users
- **Fedora** – Advanced users

---

## 💻 How to Migrate from Windows to Linux (Step-by-Step)

### 1. Backup Your Data (*sauvegarde essentielle*)
Always save your files before starting.

### 2. Create a Bootable USB
Use tools like Rufus or Etcher.

### 3. Try Linux (Live Mode)
Test before installing.

### 4. Install Linux
Choose:
- Dual boot (*double démarrage*)
- Full migration

---

## 🇫🇷 Important Settings for France

- Language → Français  
- Keyboard → AZERTY  
- Timezone → Europe/Paris
